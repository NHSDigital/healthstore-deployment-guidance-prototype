What is HealthStore

HealthStore is an NHS service to help the safe and efficient roll out of apps that support people with long-term health conditions.

These apps are called digital therapeutics. They have been tested in clinical trials and shown to help people stay out of hospital and feel better day to day.

To be listed on HealthStore, an app must be approved by NICE (the National Institute for Health and Care Excellence).

For commissioners

HealthStore makes it easier for commissioners to find and choose approved apps for their local population.

All the apps on HealthStore have already been checked and approved. This means commissioners do not need to carry out their own checks. They can use HealthStore to:

  • browse approved apps
  • choose the right app for their area
  • sign legal and assurance agreements in one place

For clinicians

Once a commissioner has signed up to HealthStore, clinicians in that area can start prescribing apps to their patients.

HealthStore gives clinicians:

  • clear clinical pathways to follow
  • access to NICE-approved apps for their patients
  • ready-made resources to help embed the apps into their day-to-day work

For patients

If your clinician prescribes a HealthStore app, you will get access to a digital therapeutic — an app designed to help you manage a long-term health condition.

These apps can help you:

  • manage your condition yourself, at home
  • feel better in your everyday life
