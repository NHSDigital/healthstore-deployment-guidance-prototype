What is HealthStore

Healthstore is an NHS Service that supports the scaled rollout of NICE-approved digital therapeutics.

These apps help support people with long term health conditions.

Digital therapeutics have been proven in clinical studies to be effective in reducing hospital admissions and in improving patients reported quality of life.

For commissioners

The Healthstore website connects suppliers of NICE approved digital therapeutics with commissioners. It helps commissioners to:

  • access clinically proven digital therapeutics
  • understand funding models and benefit from templated legal agreements
  • embed digital therapeutics into best practice clinical pathways

Commissioners can use the Healthstore service to assess which clinically approved digital therapeutics are most suited to their population and to support their procurement and effective roll out.

For clinicians

When a commissioner enters into an agreement with Healthstore then clinicians in this territory can begin to prescribe digital therapeutics to their patients.

They can benefit from:

  • approved clinical pathways
  • access to NICE approved digital therapeutics for their patients
  • centrally-tested communications and support embedding it into their local workflows

For patients

If your clinician prescribes a HealthStore app, you will get access to a digital therapeutic — an app designed to help you manage a long-term health condition.

These apps can help you:

  • manage your condition yourself, at home
  • reduce the risk of complications related to your condition
  • feel better in your everyday life
