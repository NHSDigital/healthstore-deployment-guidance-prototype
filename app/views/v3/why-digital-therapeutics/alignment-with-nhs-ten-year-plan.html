Alignment with NHS Ten year plan

NHS HealthStore is named in the NHS ten year plan. Scaling digital therapeutics is part of a wider NHS move towards digital-first, pathway-led care. It fits in with the priorities within the NHSE National Strategy, helping shift from:

  • Sickness to prevention
  • Analogue to digital
  • Hospital to community

Not only will it help patient outcomes, but it will also be a major contributor to system productivity, workforce sustainability, operational performance, and patient outcomes.

By providing an effective 'service wrapper' and mechanisms for scaling up best practice, the NHS HealthStore service will fight the inequity described by the Tudor Hart Inverse Care Law, that can sometimes be associated with digital transformation.
