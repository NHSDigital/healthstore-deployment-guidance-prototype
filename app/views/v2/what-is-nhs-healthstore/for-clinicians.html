For clinicians

When a commissioning body begins using the HealthStore service then clinicians in this territory can begin to prescribe digital therapeutics to their patients.

They can benefit from:

  • approved clinical pathways
  • access to NICE-approved digital therapeutics for their patients
  • centrally-tested communications and support embedding it into their local workflows
