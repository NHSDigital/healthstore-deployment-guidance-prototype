For suppliers

Only trusted tools which meet the strict technical, clinical safety and governance requirements can become "HealthStore-ready". Suppliers of apps which:

  • treat the approved conditions list
  • meet NICE guidelines

can apply to onboard to be part of the NHS HealthStore website.
