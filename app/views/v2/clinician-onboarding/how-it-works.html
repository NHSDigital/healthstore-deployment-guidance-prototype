How it works

Getting started and setup with NHS HealthStore

You will be told when the commissioning organisation your practice or clinical setting is part of joins the NHS HealthStore. You may be part of the clinical conversations around what to commission and why.

Once they have commissioned a digital therapeutic(s) via the service you will be told what it is and when it will become available.

You will receive a deployment pack and toolkit to help you understand how you integrate onto the service.

A simplified NHS HealthStore workflow

There are five main components of the HealthStore service flow, which are the:

  • commissioning body
  • HealthStore service
  • clinical setting
  • NHS App
  • digital therapeutic app

Clinical and information governance

Exemplar pathways

NHS HealthStore has worked with clinical bodies and the Getting It Right First Time (GIRFT) group to develop exemplar GIRFT pathways for using a digital therapeutic for each condition covered by the NHS HealthStore Service.

Following these pathways is strongly advised but is not mandatory.

Clear clinical lead

Each pathway will need its own clinical lead.

Information governance

Clinical pathways will be subject to the normal information governance rules.

However your organisation may be able to benefit from umbrella legal agreements and data sharing agreements established between the commissioning body's and NHS HealthStore as part of its participation in the service.
