I've been prescribed a digital therapeutic – what is it?

The NHS has started to prescribe apps to help you manage long term health conditions. These are sometimes called 'digital therapeutics' and are prescribed to you as part of your care plan.

They are paid for by the NHS and free for you to use. All of the apps or 'digital therapeutics' the NHS prescribes have been through strict trials and have been recommended for use by the National Institute For Clinical Excellence (NICE).
