How digital therapeutics can help you

For chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)

A digital therapeutic app can help you manage your COPD day-to-day. You'll be asked to log your symptoms regularly.

The app will teach you the right way to use your inhaler through simple videos, since getting the technique wrong is common and can mean your medication doesn't work as well as it should.
You can also do a short questionnaire from time to time to understand how much your COPD is affecting your day-to-day life, which you can bring up at appointments.

Many apps also offer educational content and a full pulmonary rehabilitation programme of exercise and breathing videos you can follow at home, instead of travelling to in-person classes.

For cardiovascular disease (CVD)

A digital therapeutic app can support you if you're recovering from a heart problem, such as a heart attack, heart surgery, or living with heart failure.
It offers a structured rehabilitation programme of exercise and education you can follow at home, rather than attending in-person classes, helping you build back strength safely and at your own pace.

The app can help you understand what's happened to your heart and why certain lifestyle changes matter, and lets you track things like your weight, blood pressure and symptoms over time.
You'll get reminders and guidance to help you build healthier habits and reduce the risk of future problems.
