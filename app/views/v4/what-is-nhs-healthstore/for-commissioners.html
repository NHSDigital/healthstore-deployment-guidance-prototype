For commissioners

The NHS HealthStore website connects suppliers of NICE-approved digital therapeutics with commissioners. It helps commissioners to:

  • access clinically-proven digital therapeutics
  • understand funding models and benefit from templated legal agreements
  • embed digital therapeutics into best practice clinical pathways

The NHS HealthStore service can help commissioning bodies assess which digital therapeutics are most suited to their population. It can also support their procurement and effective roll out.
