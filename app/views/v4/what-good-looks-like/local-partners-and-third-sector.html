Make the most of local partners and third sector organisations to help deliver digital inclusion work

When the Cornwall and Scilly ICB were running a 6 month campaign to get more patients onto pathways using digital therapeutics to help manage their COPD, they took a multi-agency approach.

By working with the local council, charities, and primary and secondary care, they were able to reach more patients and offer them tailored support. There are also national charities which help with digital inclusion support.

  • Good Things Foundation
  • Digital Poverty Alliance
