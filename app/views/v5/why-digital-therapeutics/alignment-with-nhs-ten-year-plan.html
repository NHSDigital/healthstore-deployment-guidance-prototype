Alignment with The NHS 10-Year Health Plan

The NHS HealthStore is named in the The NHS 10-Year Health Plan. Scaling digital therapeutics (DTx) is part of a wider NHS move towards digital-first, pathway-led care and will help support a shift from:

  • sickness to prevention
  • analogue to digital
  • hospital to community

From sickness to prevention focuses on catching illnesses early. From analogue to digital uses apps and data to put you in control of your health. From hospital to community moves care from big buildings to local hubs near your home.

In addition to improving patient outcomes, DTx will be a major contributor to system productivity, workforce sustainability, and operational performance.
