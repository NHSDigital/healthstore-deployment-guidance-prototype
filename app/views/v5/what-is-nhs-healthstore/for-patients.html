For patients

The NHS HealthStore enables patients to easily find and use NHS-approved health apps recommended by their clinician. These apps help people manage long-term health conditions on their own, including mental health conditions, heart surgery rehabilitation, and diabetes. The apps are sometimes referred to as digital therapeutics (DTx).

The NHS HealthStore helps patients by:

  • only providing safe apps checked by the National Institute for Health and Care Excellence to ensure it works properly and protects your private data
  • enabling patients to manage their own health without always needing to visit a doctor
  • enabling patients who are waiting for medical care to use these approved apps to help track and ease symptoms
  • connecting to the NHS App and enabling patients to easily link their personal health devices
