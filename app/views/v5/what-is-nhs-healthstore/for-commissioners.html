For commissioners

The NHS HealthStore connects suppliers of NICE-approved digital therapeutics with commissioners such as people working for Integrated Care Boards (ICBs).

The HealthStore helps commissioners to:

  • view a catalogue of tools evaluated for clinical and cost-effectiveness by the National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (NICE)
  • safely procure, fund, and roll out clinically-effective digital health technologies at scale
  • standardise funding and pricing, protecting NHS budgets
  • reduce health inequalities by providing patients with access to the same high-quality, NICE-approved digital tools regardless of their location, and supporting standardised care pathways
