What NHS HealthStore is

The NHS HealthStore is an upcoming digital service being developed by NHS England. It aligns with the NHS 10-Year Plan and enables NHS patients to easily access NHS-funded therapy, using the National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (NICE)-approved apps. These apps are sometimes referred to as Digital Therapeutics (DTx) and their use can help prevent, manage, or treat specific medical conditions.

The NHS HealthStore makes clinically-assured DTx easier to find, safer to use and more consistent to commission. It ensures that clinicians, commissioners and patients are well supported through each part of their journey.
