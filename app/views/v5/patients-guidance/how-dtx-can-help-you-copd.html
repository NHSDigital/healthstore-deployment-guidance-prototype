How digital therapeutics (DTx) can help you

For chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)

A digital therapeutic app can help you manage your COPD day-to-day. You'll be asked to log your symptoms regularly using the app.

The app uses videos to teach you how to use your inhaler correctly. Using your inhaler correctly will ensure that your medication works as well as possible.

You can also use the app to complete a short questionnaire that tells us how your COPD is affecting your day-to-day life. The information in your questionnaire can be discussed at your appointments.

Many apps also offer educational content and a full pulmonary rehabilitation programme of exercise and breathing videos you can follow at home, instead of travelling to in-person classes.
