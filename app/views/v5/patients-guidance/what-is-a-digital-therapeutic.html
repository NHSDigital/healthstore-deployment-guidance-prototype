Helping you understand what a Digital Therapeutic (DTx) is

Via the NHS HealthStore, the NHS prescribes apps to help you manage long-term health conditions. These apps are sometimes called digital therapeutics (DTx) and are prescribed to you by a clinician as part of your care plan. They are paid for by the NHS and free for you to use.

All the apps or DTxs the NHS prescribes have undergone rigorous trials and are authorised by the National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (NICE).
