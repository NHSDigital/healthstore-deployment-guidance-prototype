Digital therapeutics (DTx) must embed into a service or pathway

From research, we know that digital therapeutics (DTx) are most successful when embedded into a clinical pathway. Patients are referred to a clinical service, and the DTx app is built into that service.

When patients are introduced to DTx, but do not receive ongoing support in using them, the benefits to them are often unclear. An unsupported rollout also poses the risk of widening health inequalities.

"We refer to a service from general practice. We don't even talk about the technology so much. The therapeutic is an option within an established pathway."

"Bad deployment of technology exacerbates health inequalities. When you go fully digital – the danger is people just vanish. We saw that in COVID."

Dr James Cowling, Roxton Practice, North East Lincolnshire

At Roxton, a DTx became part of the COPD pathway, led by one named respiratory consultant. Other clinicians can refer patients to that pathway. The team also built additional support for patients with low digital confidence.
